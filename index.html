Generalised Dot Maps

Notes:

Currently, this simply plots all dots in the visible range, which is why it is slow at small scales. When zooming in, there are fewer dots and it gets smoother.
On zoom and/or pan, the underlying map is zoomed with svg zoom (faster than reprojecting). Then, the projection is updated and the dots are reprojected.
The dataset shows all Starbucks stores worldwide (Source).
